Polygraph Machine

Description:
A circuit that measures your heartbeat whenever it rises or falls and, dependently, specific light bulb flashes on for feedback, triggering a buzzer, and the OLED Display shows the heartbeat rates.

How to Use?
You place your finger on the pulse rate sensor and let it measure your heartbeat rate. The OLED then displays the BPM (Beats per Minute) and depending on the conditions, the BPM reaches a level higher than 100, the buzzer is then triggered, the red LED turns on, and the White LED proceeds to blink at a speed dependent on the BPM levels.
